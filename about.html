About One Plant a Day

Our Mission

One Plant a Day is dedicated to fostering a deeper connection between people and the natural world through daily plant education. We believe that learning about plants can bring peace, wonder, and environmental awareness to your everyday life.

  • Daily plant discoveries with fascinating facts
  • Beautiful imagery to inspire and educate
  • No ads, no tracking, just pure plant knowledge
  • Building environmental awareness one plant at a time
