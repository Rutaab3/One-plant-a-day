Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

Find answers to common questions about One Plant a Day, our mission, and how to make the most of your daily plant discoveries.

One Plant a Day is a daily educational platform that features a different plant each day with fascinating facts, beautiful imagery, and interesting details about its habitat, uses, and unique characteristics.

Our goal is to foster a deeper connection between people and the natural world through accessible, engaging plant education.

We feature a new plant every single day! Our content is automatically updated daily, so you'll always have something new to discover when you visit.

You can also browse previous days using our calendar feature to explore plants you might have missed.

No! One Plant a Day is completely free and requires no registration, login, or personal information.

Simply visit our website anytime to discover today's featured plant. No barriers, no hassle.

Absolutely not! We believe in privacy-first design. One Plant a Day:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not use tracking cookies
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not require user accounts

Our mission is pure education and inspiration, not data collection or monetization.

All our plant information is carefully researched and verified from reputable botanical sources, scientific databases, and educational institutions.

We ensure accuracy while making the content accessible and engaging for all knowledge levels.

Yes! One Plant a Day is fully responsive and optimized for all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

The experience is designed to be beautiful and functional regardless of your screen size.

We love hearing from plant enthusiasts! While we don't currently have a formal submission process, we're always interested in learning about fascinating plants.

Feel free to reach out through our contact information if you have suggestions or interesting plant stories to share.

One Plant a Day is built with modern web technologies:

  • HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript for the frontend
  • Bootstrap for responsive design
  • AOS (Animate On Scroll) for smooth animations
  • Google Sheets API for content management

We prioritize performance, accessibility, and user experience in our technical choices.
